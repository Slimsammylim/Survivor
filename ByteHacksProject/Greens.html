Wild Greens

This is a quick guide to help you find edible greens!

Inedible Plants

How to tell if a plant is NOT safe to eat:

NOT SAFE:

  • Plants with shiny leaves
  • Plants with a bad odor
  • Plants with a bitter or soapy flavor
  • Plants that have a milky sap in the stems
  • Plants growing near contaminated water

Wood Sorrel (edible)



The Wood Sorrel is easy to identify, just look for the signature three heart shaped leaves. The flowers, leaves,and immature seed pods are edible. The plant's taste also has hint of lemon.

Dandelions (edible)



Dandelions are common throught the US. You can eat the everything from the flower to root of the dandilion. This plant has a distinct, tasty, and beautiful yellow flower.
