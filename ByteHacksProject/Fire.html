Making a Fire

This is a quick guide on how to make a fire (without matches or a lighter)!

Materials

What you need:

  • Two pieces of dry wood
  • A knife
  • Kindling wood (sticks, twigs, and leaves ideally made of soft wood such as pine, fir, or cedar)

Steps

Written Steps:

  1. Split a dried stick vertically.
  2. Carve out the inside of the stick that was halved.
  3. Take another dried stick, and use a knife to carve and sharpen the end of it.
  4. Place the halved stick on the groud, divit facing up.
  5. Rub the shapened end of the stick into the divit in the halfed stick.
  6. When you see smoke and theres a buildup of a dark powder, pour the powder on the kindling.
  7. Blow on the kindling and wave it from side to side to start the fire.
